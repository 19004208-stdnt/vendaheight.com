About Us

Our Mission

We are committed to providing the best possible living experience for our residents. Our goal is to create a welcoming and comfortable environment that feels like home.

We strive to provide exceptional customer service and respond to the needs of our residents in a timely and efficient manner.

Our History

Vendaheight has been providing quality housing since 2005. We started with a single apartment complex and have since expanded to several locations throughout the country.

Throughout our history, we have remained committed to our core values of integrity, quality, and customer service. We are proud of the relationships we have built with our residents and look forward to continuing to serve them for many years to come.
